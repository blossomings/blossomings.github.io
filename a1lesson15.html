Places in Town

Objectives
➧ Name common places in a town
➧ Use prepositions to describe where things are
➧ Ask and answer basic questions about locations


Vocabulary – Places in Town



Prepositions of Place

➧ In
➧ On
➧ Under
➧ Next to
➧ Between
➧ In front of
➧ Behind
➧ Near

Examples
➧ The dog is under the table.
➧ The café is next to the bank.
➧ The school is between the library and the park.


Grammar – Prepositions

Structure
[Place A] is [preposition] [Place B].

Examples
➧ The hospital is behind the park.

➧ The bus stop is in front of the school.

➧ The post office is near the supermarket.
