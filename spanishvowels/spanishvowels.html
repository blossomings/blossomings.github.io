The Five Spanish Vowels

Las Vocales | Vowels



Lesson Overview

The five Spanish vowels are the foundation of Spanish reading. Unlike English, each Spanish vowel has one consistent sound, making Spanish much easier to decode and pronounce.

By learning these five vowel sounds, students can begin reading hundreds of simple Spanish words.


Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to
➧ Recognize the five Spanish vowels.
➧ Pronounce each vowel correctly.
➧ Identify vowel sounds in simple words.
➧ Read simple vowel-only syllables.
➧ Build confidence reading basic Spanish words.
