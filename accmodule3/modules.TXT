Format
lesson objectives, vocabulary, notes, activities, case study, quiz



Module 1: Foundations of Accounting (Classes 1–15)
1. Introduction to Accounting

Objectives: Understand what accounting is and why it matters.
Topics: Definition, history, purpose, bookkeeping vs accounting.
Practice: Identify accounting in real-life scenarios.
Assignment: Short reflection: “Why accounting is essential in business.”

2. Users & Purposes of Accounting

Topics: Internal vs external users, decision-making.
Practice: Match users to the type of information they need.
Assessment: Quick quiz.

3. Business Types & Accounting Assumptions

Topics: Sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation; economic entity, going concern, time period.
Practice: Choose the correct assumption in scenarios.

4. Accounting Principles (GAAP/IFRS)

Topics: Consistency, prudence, matching, IFRS vs GAAP differences.
Activity: Mini case study comparison.

5. Accounting Equation & Real-Life Scenarios

Practice: Expand and apply the equation to daily business transactions.
Assessment: Equation worksheet.

6. Double-Entry System Basics

Topics: Why double entry exists; debit/credit logic.
Practice: Analyze simple transactions.

7. Debit/Credit Masterclass

Topics: Rules for assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, expenses.
Practice: Debit/credit drills.

8. Chart of Accounts

Topics: Account numbering, structure, customization.
Practice: Create a chart of accounts for a sample business.

9. Journals – Special & General

Topics: Sales journal, purchases journal, cash receipts, cash payments.
Practice: Journal entries for a small business.

10. Ledger Posting

Topics: Posting from journal to ledger; T-accounts.
Practice: Prepare ledgers.

11. Trial Balance

Topics: Purpose; detecting errors.
Practice: Create a trial balance from ledgers.

12. Accounting Errors & Suspense Accounts

Topics: Types of errors, error correction.
Practice: Correct supplied errors.

13. Accrual vs Cash Accounting

Topics: Timing differences; revenue recognition.
Practice: Convert cash to accrual.

14. Adjusting Entries (Part 1)

Topics: Prepaid expenses, unearned revenue.
Practice: Adjusting entry worksheet.

15. Adjusting Entries (Part 2)

Topics: Accrued revenue, accrued expenses, depreciation basics.
Assessment: End-of-module test.

Module 2: Completing the Accounting Cycle (Classes 16–25)
16. Adjusted Trial Balance

Practice: Prepare ATB from unadjusted plus adjustments.

17. Income Statement

Topics: Format, multi-step vs single-step.
Practice: Build an income statement.

18. Balance Sheet

Topics: Classified vs unclassified.
Activity: Prepare a classified balance sheet.

19. Statement of Owner’s Equity

Topics: Capital, drawings/withdrawals.
Practice: Complete SOE for a small business.

20. Statement of Cash Flows (Intro)

Method: Indirect vs direct.
Practice: Operating activities adjustments.

21. Comprehensive Financial Statement Preparation

Activity: Prepare all four statements from a trial balance.

22. Closing Entries

Topics: Closing revenue, expense, income summary.
Practice: Journalize and post.

23. Post-Closing Trial Balance

Topics: Purpose, accounts remaining.
Activity: Create PCTB.

24. Full Cycle Practice Quiz

Assessment: Full cycle MCQ + practical.

25. Mini-Project Submission

Students complete a full accounting cycle for a fictional business.

Module 3: Cash, Receivables, Payables (Classes 26–35)
26. Cash Controls

Topics: Internal controls, cash handling.
Practice: Identify weak controls.

27. Bank Reconciliation

Practice: Prepare reconciliation from statement + ledger.

28. Petty Cash Systems

Activity: Petty cash vouchers and replenishment entries.

29. Accounts Receivable

Topics: Recognition, sales on credit.
Practice: Credit sales journal.

30. Notes Receivable

Topics: Promissory notes, maturity date.
Practice: Calculate interest.

31. Allowance for Doubtful Accounts

Methods: Percent of AR, percent of sales, aging method.
Practice: Create allowance entries.

32. Accounts Payable

Topics: Vendor accounts; control.
Practice: AP aging schedule.

33. Notes Payable

Topics: Negotiating terms, interest.
Practice: Prepare amortization schedule.

34. Interest Calculations & Amortization

Activity: Loan amortization tables.

35. Credit Policies & Collection Management

Topics: Risk assessment; credit scoring.
Assessment: Case study.

Module 4: Inventory & Costing (Classes 36–50)
36. Inventory Systems

Topics: Perpetual vs periodic.
Practice: Compare COGS results.

37. FIFO
38. LIFO
39. Weighted Average

For each lesson:
Topics: Calculation + income effects.
Practice: Apply method to given inventory data.

40. Retail Inventory Method

Practice: Estimate ending inventory.

41. Inventory Errors & Effects

Topics: Income statement and balance sheet impacts.
Activity: Error correction problems.

42. Merchandise Transactions

Practice: Purchase and sales entries under periodic/perpetual.

43. Cost of Goods Sold

Topics: Formula, calculation.
Practice: COGS worksheet.

44. Stock Count & Inventory Controls

Topics: Cycle counts, shrinkage.
Activity: Identify control weaknesses.

45. Introduction to Cost Accounting

Topics: Types of costs, direct vs indirect.
Practice: Classify costs.

46. Job Order Costing

Practice: Job cost sheets.

47. Process Costing

Topics: Equivalent units, weighted average.
Practice: Compute EUP.

48. Activity-Based Costing

Activity: Allocate overhead using cost drivers.

49. Overhead Allocation

Topics: Traditional vs refined methods.
Practice: Allocate overhead to departments.

50. Cost-Volume-Profit Analysis

Topics: BEP, margin of safety.
Practice: Break-even charts.

Module 5: Fixed Assets, Equity, Advanced Liabilities (Classes 51–65)
51. Classification of Assets

Activity: Identify asset categories.

52. Capitalization vs Expense

Practice: Decide which items to capitalize.

53. Depreciation – Straight Line

Practice: Depreciation schedule.

54. Depreciation – Declining Balance & Units of Production

Practice: Apply DB and UOP.

55. Revaluation Model (IFRS)

Topics: Revaluation surplus, impairment.
Practice: Revaluation entries.

56. Asset Disposal

Topics: Gain/loss recognition.
Practice: Disposal problems.

57. Impairment

Topics: Recoverable amount; write-down.
Practice: Impairment calculations.

58. Natural Resources & Depletion

Practice: Depletion rate & expense.

59. Intangibles & Amortization

Topics: Patents, goodwill.
Practice: Amortization schedules.

60. Current Liabilities

Topics: Wages payable, taxes payable, unearned revenue.

61. Long-Term Liabilities

Topics: Notes, leases, bonds.

62. Bonds – Issuance & Interest

Practice: Issue at par.

63. Bonds – Premium/Discount Amortization

Practice: Effective interest method.

64. Shareholders’ Equity

Topics: Shares, paid-in capital.

65. Dividends & Stock Splits

Practice: Entries + effects on equity.

Module 6: Financial Analysis & Managerial Accounting (Classes 66–75)
66. Horizontal Analysis

Practice: Trend percentages.

67. Vertical Analysis

Activities: Common-size statements.

68. Ratio Analysis – Liquidity

Topics: CR, QR, AR turnover.

69. Ratio Analysis – Profitability

Topics: GPM, NPM, ROA, ROE.

70. Ratio Analysis – Solvency

Topics: Debt ratio, times interest earned.

71. Budgeting & Master Budget

Practice: Sales, production, cash budgets.

72. Variance Analysis

Topics: Material, labor, overhead variances.
Practice: Compute variances.

73. Decision-Making with Accounting Info

Topics: Make-or-buy, drop/keep.

74. Relevant Costing

Case Study: Special order decisions.

75. Capital Budgeting (NPV/IRR Basics)

Practice: Evaluate two investment options.

Module 7: Professional Tools, Ethics & Software (Classes 76–80)
76. Internal Controls & Fraud Prevention

Topics: Segregation of duties; fraud triangle.
Case Study: Identify fraud risks.

77. Taxation Basics

Topics: Income tax, VAT, withholding, tax returns.
Practice: Simple tax computations.

78. Excel for Accounting

Practice: Build templates for:

Depreciation

VAT return

Inventory

Cash flows (indirect method)

79. Accounting Software – Full Cycle Demo

You may use: QuickBooks, Zoho Books, Tally, Sage, or Wave.
Activity: Students complete a small business cycle.

80. Final Project Presentation

Students prepare and submit a full accounting portfolio, including:
✔ Financial statements

✔ Budget
