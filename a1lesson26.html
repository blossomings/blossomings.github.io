Final Review

Congratulations!
This is your final lesson in the A1 course!

Today you will:
• Review important vocabulary and grammar
• Practice speaking and writing
• Take a short quiz
• Reflect on your progress

Review – What You Have Learned

Vocabulary topics:
• Greetings and introductions
• Daily routine, time, and activities
• Food, clothes, weather, and health
• Places, jobs, furniture, holidays, and more

Grammar structures:
• Present Simple & Present Continuous
• There is · There are
• Can · Can’t
• Past Simple (regular & irregular verbs)
• Comparatives and Superlatives
• Demonstrative Pronouns
• Suggestions and invitations

Speaking Review – Talk About Yourself

Practice aloud:
• What’s your name?
• Where are you from?
• What do you do every day?
• What do you do on weekends?
• What’s your favorite place? Why?
• What did you do last weekend?

Writing Practice – About Me

Task:
Write a short paragraph (5–6 sentences)

Model:
My name is Sara. I’m from Egypt. I wake up at 7:00 and go to work. I like cooking and shopping. My favorite place is the beach. Last weekend, I visited my friend and watched a movie.

Final Quiz – Vocabulary

1. We eat cake on a ___.
A) hospital
B) birthday
C) furniture

2. I wear ___ when it's cold.
A) shorts
B) mitts
C) sandals

3. A ___ works in a hospital.
A) teacher
B) doctor
C) farmer

Final Quiz – Grammar

1. This book is ___ than that one.
A) gooder
B) better
C) more good

2. There ___ a table in the room.
A) is
B) are
C) be

3. Yesterday, I ___ to the market.
A) go
B) goes
C) went

• I can describe places and people.
• I can write a short paragraph.
• I understand basic past and present tenses.
