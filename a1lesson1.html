Greetings and Introductions

Objectives
• Use greetings and farewells
• Introduce ourselves
• Ask for someone’s name, age, and country
• Respond in a short conversation

Greetings

• Hello
• Hi
• Good morning
• Good afternoon
• Good evening

Farewells

• Goodbye
• Bye
• See you
• Good night

Introducing Yourself

• My name is...
• I’m (name)
• I’m from (country)
• I’m ___ years old

Example:
My name is Anna. I’m from Spain. I’m 22 years old.

Asking Questions

• What’s your name?
• Where are you from?
• How old are you?
