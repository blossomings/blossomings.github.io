A Patient’s Appeal for Hope

My Story
My name is Mattar, and I am fighting for my life against lymphoma (blood cancer).

Over the past few months, my journey has been filled with endless hospital visits, painful procedures, and mounting bills. To diagnose my condition, I went through:

➛ CT Scan
➛ Biopsy
➛ PET Scan
➛ IHC Test
➛ Echo Test
➛ Kidney Function Test
➛ Multiple Blood Tests

Once diagnosed, I began my treatment with the first cycle of R-CHOP chemotherapy. I also had to undergo a lung fluid drainage procedure "Pleural effusion", which was both frightening and exhausting.


My Struggle
These treatments have drained me not only physically and emotionally, but also financially. My family and I have spent everything we had - savings, loans, and borrowed help from relatives - just to reach this stage of treatment.

Now, I stand at a critical point.

➛ Without further chemotherapy cycles and continued care, my fight against lymphoma cannot continue.

➛ But I have no money left to pay for the next stage of treatment.


My Appeal
I am humbly asking for your help. Your donation - big or small - can directly go towards:

➛ Completing the next chemotherapy cycles

➛ Covering hospital bills, medication, and tests

➛ Supporting my daily nutrition and care

Each day matters. Each contribution brings me closer to healing.
