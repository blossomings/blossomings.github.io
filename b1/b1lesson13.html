Passive Voice

Objectives
Grammar Focus
➧ Passive voice | present and past

Vocabulary
➧ News-related vocabulary

Practice
➧ Discussing recent news stories and current events


Grammar Focus

Present Passive
Is | Are + Past participle
➧ The news is reported daily.

Past Passive
Was | Were + Past participle
➧ The story was written last week.

When to Use
Focus on the action or event rather than the subject
➧ The building was constructed in 2020.

Wrap-Up

Homework
Find a news article and rewrite three sentences from the article in the passive voice

Example

Original Sentence
➧ The government passed the new law last week.

Passive Sentence
➧ The new law was passed by the government last week.
