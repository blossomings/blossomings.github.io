Habits and Routines

Objectives

Review:
Present Simple and Present Continuous

Vocabulary:
Daily routines and frequency adverbs

Practice:
Describing and comparing routines

Grammar Focus

Present Simple:
Regular actions or routines
➢ I wake up at 7 AM.

Present Continuous:
Actions happening right now
➢ I am eating breakfast.

Vocabulary

Daily routines:
◦ Wake up
◦ Brush teeth
◦ Go to work · school
◦ Have lunch
◦ Exercise
◦ Go to bed

Frequency adverbs:
◦ Always
◦ Usually
◦ Often
◦ Sometimes
◦ Rarely
◦ Never

Wrap-Up

Homework:
Write a short paragraph about your daily routine using both present simple and present continuous

Example:
I usually wake up at 7 AM and have breakfast. Right now, I am working on my English homework. I often go to the gym in the evening, but today I am resting because I am tired.
