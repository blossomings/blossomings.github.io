Future Plans and Predictions

Objectives

Grammar Focus:
Future with "will, Going to, and Present continuous"

Vocabulary:
Future events and predictions

Practice:
Making plans

Grammar Focus

Will:
For spontaneous decisions or predictions
➢ I will call you later.

Going to:
For plans and intentions
➢ I’m going to study tomorrow.

Present Continuous:
For fixed plans
➢ I’m meeting my friend at 6 PM.

Vocabulary

Future Events:
◦ Graduation
◦ Vacation
◦ Party
◦ Job interview

Predictions:
◦ Success
◦ Improvement
◦ Change
◦ Challenge

Wrap-Up

Homework:
Write a paragraph about your plans for the next month, using all three future forms

Example:
Next month, I am going to start a new fitness routine. I am meeting my trainer next week to discuss the plan. I think I will feel more energetic after a few weeks. I’m also going to visit my family for a weekend trip.
