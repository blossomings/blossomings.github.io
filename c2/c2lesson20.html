Course Recap

Objective

Review the major concepts, vocabulary, and skills covered throughout the course.

➢ Key Areas to Recap
Advanced Grammar:
Complex sentence structures, nuanced use of modals, and conditionals.

Vocabulary:
Precision and nuance in word choice, idiomatic language, and cultural references.

Writing Skills:
Techniques for formal and informal registers, synthesizing knowledge in extended writing.

Speaking and Listening:
Pronunciation, intonation, persuasive techniques, and engaging in advanced discussions.

➢ Example Recap
Grammar Focus:
Review the use of inversion for emphasis, as in
“Rarely have I seen such dedication.”

Vocabulary Focus:

Final Self-Assessment

Self-Assessment Questions

Grammar:
Can I confidently use complex structures in my writing and speaking?

Vocabulary:
Have I expanded my vocabulary with precise, nuanced words and idiomatic expressions?

Writing:
Am I able to write extended pieces with clear organization and coherence?

Speaking and Listening:
Do I speak with near-native pronunciation and intonation? Can I engage in advanced discussions effectively?
