International Business and Global Strategies

Today's objectives are to

➧ Understand the fundamentals of international business and global trade.
➧ Explore strategies for entering and expanding in global markets.
➧ Discuss cultural considerations and challenges in international business.


Introduction to International Business

International business involves commercial activities that
➧ span across national borders
➧ encompassing imports
➧ exports
➧ foreign direct investment
➧ and global supply chains.


Importance of International Business

Market Expansion
➧ Accessing new markets and diversifying revenue streams.

Resource Acquisition
➧ Sourcing materials, technology, and talent globally.

Risk Diversification
➧ Spreading business risks across different economies and markets.

Competitive Advantage
➧ Gaining competitive edge through innovation and global presence.

Economic Growth
➧ Contributing to global economic development and prosperity.


Strategies for Entering Global Markets

Exporting
➧ Selling products or services to foreign markets.

Licensing and Franchising
➧ Allowing foreign entities to use intellectual property or business models.

Joint Ventures and Strategic Alliances
➧ Partnering with local companies to enter new markets.

Foreign Direct Investment
➧ Establishing subsidiaries or acquiring stakes in foreign businesses.

E-commerce
➧ Leveraging online platforms to reach international customers.


Cultural Considerations in International Business

Communication Styles
➧ Understanding differences in language
➧ non-verbal cues
➧ and communication norms.

Business Etiquette
➧ Adapting to local customs and protocols in business interactions.

Values and Beliefs
➧ Respecting cultural values and ethical standards in business practices.

Negotiation Styles
➧ Recognizing variations in negotiation approaches and strategies.

Workplace Culture
➧ Adapting management and leadership styles to cultural expectations.


Challenges in International Business

Legal and Regulatory Compliance
➧ Navigating different laws, regulations, and trade policies.

Logistics and Supply Chain Management
➧ Managing complex global supply chains and distribution networks.

Currency Fluctuations
➧ Dealing with exchange rate risks and financial volatility.

Political Instability
➧ Addressing geopolitical risks and uncertainties in foreign markets.

Competitive Pressures
➧ Facing local and global competitors in diverse market environments.


Globalization and Technology Trends

Digital Transformation
➧ Leveraging technology for global connectivity
➧ e-commerce
➧ and data analytics.

Virtual Collaboration
➧ Facilitating remote work and cross-border team collaboration.

Supply Chain Innovation
➧ Adopting automation
➧ AI
➧ and IoT for efficient supply chain management.

Market Intelligence
Using big data and predictive analytics to understand global market trends.

Cybersecurity
➧ Protecting data and information in global operations.
