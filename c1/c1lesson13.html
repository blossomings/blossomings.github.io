Summarizing and Paraphrasing Complex Texts

Objectives

Summarizing:
Condensing information while retaining the main ideas.

Paraphrasing:
Rewriting text in your own words without changing the meaning.

Importance:
Key skills for academic and professional writing.

Techniques

Summarizing Tips

Identify the main idea and supporting details.
➢ Focus on the core message of the text.

Omit unnecessary information.
➢ Remove examples, anecdotes, or any irrelevant details.

Keep it concise.
➢ A summary should be significantly shorter than the original text, capturing only the essentials.

Techniques

Paraphrasing Tips

Use synonyms and different sentence structures..
➢ Change the wording and structure while preserving the meaning.

Keep the original meaning.
➢ Make sure your paraphrase conveys the same ideas as the original.
