• Talk about your past activities (yesterday, last week)
• Form positive and negative sentences in the past simple

Grammar – Past Simple

Structure:
➢ Subject +
➢ Verb-ed (past form)

Time Expressions for the Past
Yesterday
Last week
Last year
Two days ago
In 2020

Examples:
I studied English yesterday.

She watched a movie last night.

They visited the museum last week.

Grammar – Negative Sentences

Structure:
➢s; Subject +
➢ didn’t +
➢ base verb

Examples:
I didn’t go to school yesterday.
He didn’t play football.
We didn’t do our homework.

Yes · No Questions in the Past

Structure:
➢ Did +
➢ subject +
➢ base verb?

Examples:
Q. Did you study yesterday?
A. Yes, I did. · No, I didn’t.

Q. Did she go to class?
A. Yes, she did. · No, she didn’t.
