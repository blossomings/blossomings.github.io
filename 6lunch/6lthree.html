Lunch Plan - 6 Months Old

Week 3

Introducing solids to your 6-month-old can be a joyful and nourishing experience. This 4-week lunch plan focuses on simple, nutritious, and age-appropriate purees and mashed foods. Each meal is designed with single or gently combined ingredients for easy digestion and allergen monitoring. Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

Tips Before You Start:
⁕ Offer only 1 new food every 3 days to watch for allergies.
⁕ Make sure the texture is smooth and suitable for early eaters.
⁕ Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

How to Use This Plan:
Use fresh ingredients.
Offer 2–3 tablespoons per meal depending on baby’s appetite.
Store extra portions in sterilized containers.
Adjust consistency with breastmilk, formula, or boiled water.




Lunch Recipe
Spinach & Potato Purée

Ingredients
½ small potato (peeled and chopped)
A handful of spinach leaves

Instructions
Steam the potato until halfway cooked, then add spinach and steam both until soft. Blend together with water until smooth.




Lunch Recipe
Apple, Carrot & Quinoa Purée

Ingredients
2 tbsp cooked quinoa
½ apple (steamed)
1 small carrot (steamed)

Instructions
Blend quinoa, apple, and carrot until smooth. Add water if needed.




Lunch Recipe
Lentil & Sweet Potato Purée

Ingredients
2 tbsp cooked red lentils
¼ cup steamed sweet potato

Instructions
Blend lentils and sweet potato together until smooth.




Lunch Recipe
Beetroot & Apple Purée

Ingredients
½ small beetroot
½ small apple

Instructions
Steam beetroot and apple until soft. Blend into a rich, colorful puree.




Lunch Recipe
Pumpkin & Apple

Ingredients
½ cup steamed pumpkin
½ steamed apple
A pinch of Ceylon cinnamon (optional)

Instructions
Blend all ingredients until smooth. Cinnamon adds flavor but is optional at 6 months.




Lunch Recipe
Rice & Green Bean Purée

Ingredients
2 tbsp cooked white rice
½ cup green beans

Instructions
Steam green beans. Blend with rice and a bit of water until smooth.




Lunch Recipe
Blueberry & Banana Mash

Ingredients
2 tbsp blueberries
½ banana

Instructions
Steam blueberries (optional for easier digestion). Mash together with banana.
