Seasonal Fruit & Vegetable Calendar for Baby Food (6+ Months)


Note: Seasons may vary slightly depending on location. This guide uses a general temperate climate.

Tips for Using This Calendar:
⁕ Introduce one new food at a time, waiting 3 days to observe any allergies.
⁕ Start with single-ingredient purees before introducing combinations.
⁕ Choose organic, local, and seasonal whenever possible for maximum nutrition.
⁕ Always steam or cook hard fruits and vegetables to soften them for 6-month-old babies.
