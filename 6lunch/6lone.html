Lunch Plan - 6 Months Old

Week 1

Introducing solids to your 6-month-old can be a joyful and nourishing experience. This 4-week lunch plan focuses on simple, nutritious, and age-appropriate purees and mashed foods. Each meal is designed with single or gently combined ingredients for easy digestion and allergen monitoring. Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

Tips Before You Start:
⁕ Offer only 1 new food every 3 days to watch for allergies.
⁕ Make sure the texture is smooth and suitable for early eaters.
⁕ Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

How to Use This Plan:
Use fresh ingredients.
Offer 2–3 tablespoons per meal depending on baby’s appetite.
Store extra portions in sterilized containers.
Adjust consistency with breastmilk, formula, or boiled water.




Lunch Recipe
Zucchini Purée

Ingredients
1 small zucchini (peeled and chopped)

Instructions
Steam until soft. Blend into a smooth puree. Add a bit of water if needed.




Lunch Recipe
Butternut Squash Purée

Ingredients
½ cup peeled, cubed butternut squash

Instructions
Steam until soft. Blend into a smooth puree.




Lunch Recipe
Apple & Carrot Purée

Ingredients
1 apple
1 small carrot

Instructions
Steam both together. Blend until smooth.




Lunch Recipe
Pea Purée

Ingredients
½ cup peas (fresh or frozen)

Instructions
Steam peas until soft. Blend with water or breastmilk.




Lunch Recipe
Green Bean Purée

Ingredients
½ cup green beans

Instructions
Steam until soft. Blend with water.




Lunch Recipe
Apple & Zucchini Blend

Ingredients
½ apple
½ zucchini

Instructions
Steam together and blend.




Lunch Recipe
Rice & Carrot Purée

Ingredients
2 tbsp cooked rice
1 small steamed carrot

Instructions
Blend rice and carrot together with a little water or breastmilk.
