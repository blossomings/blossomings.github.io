Chicken Life Cycle


Objectives

➧ Understand the 6 stages of a chicken’s life cycle
➧ Identify and describe each stage
➧ Sequence the chicken life cycle correctly
➧ Learn how a chick grows inside an egg
➧ Learn why chickens are important


Introduction

Hi friends!
➧ Today we are learning about a farm animal… the chicken!
➧ Did you know that before a chick hatches, it grows inside an egg for many days?
➧ A chicken's life cycle has six exciting stages. We can watch it grow from a tiny egg into a full-grown chicken!
➧ Let's discover each stage together!


Warm-up Questions

➧ Have you ever seen a chicken?
➧ Have you ever held an egg?
➧ Have you ever seen a baby chick?


The Chicken Life Cycle

Chickens grow in stages


1. Egg

➧ A mother hen lays an egg.
➧ The egg has everything the baby chicken needs to grow.
➧ The hen keeps the egg warm.

Key idea
➧ The chicken's life begins inside an egg.


2. Embryo

➧ Inside the egg, a tiny embryo begins to grow.
➧ The embryo slowly develops a beak, wings, legs, and eyes.
➧ It uses nutrients from the yolk for food.

Key idea
➧ The baby chicken develops inside the egg.


3. Hatchling

➧ After about 21 days, the baby chicken cracks the shell.
➧ This process is called hatching.
➧ The hatchling works hard to break out of the egg.

Key idea
➧ The hatchling emerges from the egg.


4. Chick

➧ The chick is now fully out of the shell.
➧ It is small, fluffy, and covered with soft down feathers.
➧ The chick eats, drinks, and grows quickly.

Key idea
➧ The chick begins life outside the egg.


5. Young Chicken

➧ The chick grows larger.
➧ Soft down feathers are replaced by adult feathers.
➧ The young chicken learns to scratch the ground and find food.

Key idea
➧ The young chicken grows stronger and more independent.


6. Adult Chicken

➧ The chicken becomes fully grown.
➧ Adult hens lay eggs.
➧ Adult roosters protect the flock.
➧ The life cycle begins again when new eggs are laid.

Key idea
➧ Adult chickens reproduce and start the cycle again.


Why Chickens Are Important

➧ Chickens provide eggs
➧ They are important farm animals
➧ They help provide food for people
➧ Farmers care for chickens all around the world
