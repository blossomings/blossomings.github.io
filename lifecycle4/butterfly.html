Butterfly Life Cycle


Objectives

➧ Understand the 4 stages of a butterfly’s life cycle
➧ Identify and describe each stage
➧ Sequence the butterfly life cycle correctly
➧ Learn why butterflies are important


Introduction

Hi friends!
➧ Today we are learning about a beautiful insect… the butterfly!
➧ Did you know a butterfly changes completely as it grows?
➧ A butterfly starts as a tiny egg, becomes a caterpillar, then a chrysalis, and finally a beautiful butterfly!
➧ This amazing change is called a life cycle.
➧ Let’s discover each stage together!


Warm-up Questions

➧ Have you ever seen a butterfly?
➧ What colors were its wings?
➧ Have you seen a caterpillar on a leaf?



The Butterfly Life Cycle

Butterflies grow in stages


1. Egg

➧ A mother butterfly lays tiny eggs on leaves
➧ The eggs are very small
➧ Inside the egg, a baby caterpillar grows

Key idea
➧ The butterfly life begins in an egg


2. Caterpillar | Larva

➧ The egg hatches into a caterpillar
➧ Caterpillars eat lots of leaves
➧ They grow bigger and longer very quickly

Key idea
➧ The caterpillar eats and grows


3. Chrysalis | Pupa

➧ The caterpillar forms a chrysalis around itself
➧ Inside, the caterpillar changes slowly
➧ This stage is called metamorphosis

Key idea
➧ The butterfly changes inside the chrysalis


4. Adult Butterfly

➧ A beautiful butterfly comes out of the chrysalis
➧ It dries and spreads its wings
➧ The butterfly flies, drinks nectar, and lays eggs

Key idea
➧ The adult butterfly starts the cycle again


Why Butterflies Are Important

➧ Butterflies help flowers grow by spreading pollen
➧ They are part of nature and food chains
➧ Butterflies make gardens beautiful
➧ They help scientists learn about the environment
