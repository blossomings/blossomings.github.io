Rabbit Life Cycle


Objectives

➧ Understand the 4 stages of a rabbit’s life cycle
➧ Identify and describe each stage
➧ Sequence the rabbit life cycle correctly
➧ Learn why rabbits are important


Introduction

Hi friends!
➧ Today we are learning about a soft and fluffy animal… the rabbit!
➧ Did you know a rabbit changes as it grows?
➧ A rabbit starts as a tiny baby inside its mother, becomes a newborn kit, grows into a young rabbit, and finally becomes an adult rabbit!
➧ This amazing growth is called a life cycle.
➧ Let’s discover each stage together!


Warm-up Questions

➧ Have you ever seen a rabbit?
➧ What color was the rabbit?
➧ Have you seen a rabbit hop?
➧ What do rabbits like to eat?


The Rabbit Life Cycle

Rabbits grow in stages


1. Gestation

➧ A mother rabbit carries baby rabbits inside her body
➧ The babies grow safely before they are born
➧ This stage happens before the kits are born

Key idea
➧ The rabbit life begins inside the mother


2. Newborn | Kit

➧ A baby rabbit is called a kit
➧ Kits are tiny and cannot see when they are born
➧ They stay warm in a nest and drink milk from their mother

Key idea
➧ The newborn rabbit is small and needs care


3. Young Rabbit | Juvenile

➧ The young rabbit opens its eyes and grows soft fur
➧ It starts hopping and exploring
➧ It begins eating grass and vegetables

Key idea
➧ The young rabbit learns to hop and eat


4. Adult Rabbit

➧ The rabbit becomes fully grown
➧ Adult rabbits can run fast and care for babies
➧ They can start the life cycle again

Key idea
➧ The adult rabbit starts the cycle again


Why Rabbit Are Important

➧ Rabbits are part of nature and food chains
➧ They help spread seeds in nature
➧ Rabbits are gentle animals that many people care for
➧ Wild rabbits help keep ecosystems balanced
