R-CHOP Chemotherapy Cost Breakdown
R-CHOP is a combination of five powerful drugs used to treat lymphoma | non-Hodgkin’s lymphoma.

A patient typically needs 6-8 cycles, given once every 3 weeks.
Below is the approximate cost per cycle | prices vary by hospital and country.
