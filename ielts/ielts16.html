IELTS Speaking Practice | Mock Interviews

Review of Speaking Part 1

Overview
➧ Part 1 lasts 4-5 minutes and involves answering questions about familiar topics such as your home, family, studies, and interests.

Goals
➧ To build confidence, refine responses, and improve fluency and coherence.


Common Question Types

Personal Information
➧ Can you tell me about your hometown?

Work | Studies
➧ What is your favorite subject in school?

Hobbies and Interests
➧ Do you enjoy reading books? Why or why not?

Daily Routine
➧ What do you usually do on weekends?


Sample Questions and Answers

1. Do you enjoy your current job/studies? Why or why not?
Yes, I do enjoy my studies because they allow me to explore subjects that fascinate me, such as history and literature. I find it rewarding to learn about different cultures and historical events, and it helps me understand the world better.

2. How do you usually spend your weekends?
On weekends, I usually like to relax and spend time with my family. We might go for a hike or visit a local park. I also take the opportunity to catch up on my hobbies, such as painting or reading a good book.

3. Can you describe your favorite place in your hometown?
My favorite place in my hometown is the central park. It’s a large, green area with beautiful walking trails, a pond, and plenty of space to relax. I enjoy going there to unwind and enjoy nature, especially on sunny days.


Mock Interview 1

Interviewer | Can you tell me about your hometown?
Candidate | Sure! I’m from a small town called Harrogate, located in North Yorkshire, England. It’s known for its beautiful gardens, particularly the Valley Gardens, and its historic buildings. The town has a charming atmosphere with a mix of modern shops and traditional tea rooms. It’s a peaceful place with a strong sense of community, which I really appreciate.

Interviewer | What do you like to do in your free time?
Candidate | In my free time, I enjoy a variety of activities. One of my favorite pastimes is reading, especially mystery novels. I also like to go hiking in the local parks and nature reserves. Sometimes, I spend time cooking and trying out new recipes, which I find relaxing and enjoyable.


Mock Interview 2

Interviewer | Do you prefer studying alone or in a group? Why?
Candidate | I prefer studying in a group because it allows for interactive learning and discussion. When I study with others, I can exchange ideas and clarify doubts more effectively. However, I do occasionally study alone when I need to focus deeply on a specific topic or when I’m working on a project that requires uninterrupted concentration.

Interviewer | How do you usually spend your weekends?
Candidate | On weekends, I like to relax and unwind after a busy week. I often start by sleeping in and then enjoy a leisurely breakfast. I usually spend some time outdoors, whether it's going for a walk in the park or visiting a local market. I also like to catch up on my hobbies, such as painting or gardening, and spend time with family and friends.


Mock Interview 3

Interviewer | What kind of music do you like?
Candidate | I have a diverse taste in music. I enjoy listening to classical music, especially pieces by composers like Beethoven and Mozart, as it helps me relax and focus. I also appreciate contemporary genres such as pop and indie rock. Depending on my mood, I might listen to upbeat songs while exercising or soothing tunes when I’m unwinding at home.

Interviewer | Do you enjoy cooking? Why or why not?
Candidate | Yes, I do enjoy cooking. I find it a creative and rewarding activity. Cooking allows me to experiment with different ingredients and recipes, and I love the satisfaction of preparing a delicious meal from scratch. It’s also a great way to unwind and take a break from daily routines. Plus, sharing a home-cooked meal with family and friends is always enjoyable.


Mock Interview 4

Interviewer | What do you find most challenging about your studies/work?
Candidate | One of the most challenging aspects of my studies is managing time effectively, especially when juggling multiple assignments and deadlines. Balancing coursework with other responsibilities requires careful planning and organization. I try to stay on top of my schedule by using a planner and setting aside specific times for study sessions. This approach helps me stay focused and manage my workload better.

Interviewer | What are your future career plans?
Candidate | My future career plan is to work in the field of digital marketing. I’m passionate about using technology to create engaging marketing campaigns and analyzing data to improve strategies. I plan to gain experience through internships and relevant projects during my studies and eventually pursue a role where I can contribute to innovative marketing solutions.
