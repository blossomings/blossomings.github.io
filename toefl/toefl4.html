Speaking Strategies

Structure:
4 tasks
20 minutes total.

Task Types

Independent Tasks:
Express opinions on familiar topics.

Integrated Tasks:
Respond to questions based on reading and listening materials.

Evaluation Criteria:
Delivery, language use, and topic development.


Key Speaking Strategies

Organization:
Structure responses clearly with an introduction, body, and conclusion.

Clarity and Fluency:
Speak clearly and at a steady pace; avoid filler words.

Practice Speaking:
Regularly practice speaking on various topics and record yourself.

Answer All Parts:
Ensure you address all parts of the question.


Speaking Exercise

Task 1 - Independent:
Describe a memorable event from your life.
Explain why it was significant and how it has impacted you.

Instructions:
Prepare a response, focusing on clear organization and detail.

Time:
1 minute preparation
1-2 minutes speaking.

Task 2 - Integrated:
Read the following passage and then listen to a short lecture on the same topic. Respond to how they are related.

Passage:
Exercise is crucial for maintaining physical health. Regular physical activity helps in managing weight, reducing the risk of chronic diseases, and improving mental health.

Lecture Excerpt:


Instructions:
Prepare a response that integrates information from both the passage and the lecture. Focus on clarity and coherence.
