Writing Section

Task 1 - Integrated:
Read the passage below and listen to the lecture.

Passage:
Online learning has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its convenience and flexibility. Students can access a wide range of courses from anywhere in the world, and at any time. However, some critics argue that online learning lacks the personal interaction and engagement found in traditional classroom settings.

Lecture:


Write a response that summarizes the points made in the lecture and explain how they relate to the reading passage.
Task 2 - Independent:
Do you agree or disagree with the following statement:
Technology has made our lives more complicated.

Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.

Sample Answer - Integrated

This is a guideline.
The student should provide their own response.

The passage discusses the convenience of online learning but notes that it may lack personal interaction and engagement. The lecture acknowledges this concern but explains that technological advancements, such as video conferencing and discussion forums, are helping to address these issues. The lecture adds that these tools create a more interactive and collaborative learning environment, which enhances the overall online learning experience. Thus, the lecture provides solutions to the concerns raised in the passage.


Sample Answer - Independent

This is a guideline.
The student should provide their own response.

I agree with the statement that technology has made our lives more complicated. First, the constant connectivity provided by smartphones and social media often leads to information overload and increased stress, as people feel pressured to stay updated and respond quickly to messages. Second, while technology has made many tasks more efficient, it has also introduced new challenges, such as cybersecurity threats and privacy concerns. For example, people must now navigate complex settings to protect their personal information online. In conclusion, although technology offers many conveniences, it has also added layers of complexity to our daily lives.
