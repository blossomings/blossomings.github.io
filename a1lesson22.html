Rooms and Furniture

Objectives
• Name rooms and furniture
• Describe a room or house
• Use “there is · there are” correctly

Vocabulary – Rooms in a House











Vocabulary – Places of Work











Grammar – There is · There are

Use “there is” for 1 thing:
There is a sofa in the living room.
There is a bed in my bedroom.

Use “there are” for 2+ things:
There are two chairs in the kitchen.
There are many books on the shelf.

Negative Sentences

There isn’t a TV in the bedroom.

There aren’t any chairs in the bathroom.

Questions and Short Answers

Q. Is there a mirror in the bathroom?
A. Yes, there is.
A. No, there isn’t.

Q. Are there any windows in the kitchen?
A. Yes, there are.
A. No, there aren’t.
