Breakfast Plan - 6 Months Old

Week 1

Introducing solids is a special milestone! This simple 7-day breakfast plan is designed for 6-month-old babies starting their first foods. Each recipe is made with wholesome, easy-to-digest ingredients. Perfect for beginner eaters!

Tips Before You Start:
⁕ Offer only 1 new food every 3 days to watch for allergies.
⁕ Make sure the texture is smooth and suitable for early eaters.
⁕ Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

How to Use This Plan:
Use fresh ingredients.
Offer 2–3 tablespoons per meal depending on baby’s appetite.
Store extra portions in sterilized containers.
Adjust consistency with breastmilk, formula, or boiled water.




Breakfast Recipe
Rice Cereal

Description
A classic first food. Light, smooth, and easy to digest.

Ingredients
2 tbsp rice flour
1 cup water

Instructions
Boil water, stir in rice flour, cook while stirring until smooth.




Breakfast Recipe
Banana Purée

Description
Naturally sweet and creamy.
No cooking required.

Ingredients
1 ripe banana

Instructions
Mash thoroughly or blend with breastmilk for smoother texture.




Breakfast Recipe
Apple Sauce

Description
Steamed and blended for a gentle, fiber-rich breakfast.

Ingredients
1 peeled apple

Instructions
Steam until soft, then blend into a smooth purée.




Breakfast Recipe
Oatmeal Cereal

Description
Ground oats gently cooked in water or milk - a warm, nutritious start to the day.

Ingredients
2 tbsp ground oats
¾ cup water or milk

Instructions
Cook the ground oats in water or milk over low heat, stirring constantly until it becomes smooth and porridge-like. Let it cool before serving.




Breakfast Recipe
Avocado Mash

Description
Creamy and packed with healthy fats, perfect for brain development.

Ingredients
½ ripe avocado

Instructions
Scoop and mash the avocado until silky smooth. Add breastmilk or formula if you need a thinner texture.




Breakfast Recipe
Sweet Potato Purée

Description
Naturally sweet and rich in vitamins A & C - easy to digest and loved by babies.

Ingredients
1 small sweet potato

Instructions
Peel, chop, and steam the sweet potato until soft. Blend or mash to a smooth purée. Add a bit of water or milk for consistency.




Breakfast Recipe
Ragi (Finger Millet) Porridge

Description
An iron-rich, energy-boosting porridge perfect for growing babies.

Ingredients
1 tbsp ragi flour
¾ cup water

Instructions
Mix ragi flour with a little water to avoid lumps. Boil remaining water, then add the ragi paste. Stir continuously and cook until thick. Let it cool before serving.
