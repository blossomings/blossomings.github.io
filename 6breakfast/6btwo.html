Breakfast Plan - 6 Months Old

Week 2 Breakfast Meal Plan for a 6-Month-Old Baby

Introducing solids is a special milestone! This simple 7-day breakfast plan is designed for 6-month-old babies starting their first foods. Each recipe is made with wholesome, easy-to-digest ingredients. Perfect for beginner eaters!

Tips Before You Start:
⁕ Offer only 1 new food every 3 days to watch for allergies.
⁕ Make sure the texture is smooth and suitable for early eaters.
⁕ Always consult your pediatrician before introducing new foods.

How to Use This Plan:
Use fresh ingredients.
Offer 2–3 tablespoons per meal depending on baby’s appetite.
Store extra portions in sterilized containers.
Adjust consistency with breastmilk, formula, or boiled water.

Continue introducing new, wholesome ingredients while sticking to smooth, single-ingredient or gently blended meals. Remember to follow the 3-day wait rule for any new food.




Breakfast Recipe
Pear Purée

Description
Mild, juicy, and easy to digest for tiny tummies.

Ingredients
1 ripe pear (peeled and chopped)

Instructions
Steam until soft, then blend into a smooth purée. Let cool before serving.




Breakfast Recipe
Pumpkin Purée

Description
Naturally sweet and packed with vitamin A.

Ingredients
½ cup chopped pumpkin

Instructions
Steam or boil pumpkin until soft. Blend to a creamy purée, adding water/milk as needed.




Breakfast Recipe
Mashed Papaya

Description
Soft and smooth, ideal for gut health and hydration.

Ingredients
½ cup ripe papaya

Instructions
Scoop and mash until smooth. No cooking required if papaya is ripe and soft.




Breakfast Recipe
Carrot Purée

Description
A bright, slightly sweet purée rich in beta-carotene.

Ingredients
2 medium carrots (peeled and chopped)

Instructions
Steam or boil carrots until tender. Blend or mash until completely smooth.




Breakfast Recipe
Pumpkin & Apple Combo

Description
A nutritious blend of two baby-favorite flavors.

Ingredients
¼ cup chopped pumpkin
¼ cup chopped apple

Instructions
Steam both until soft, blend together into a smooth mix. Cool before feeding.




Breakfast Recipe
Rice & Moong Dal Porridge

Description
Lightly seasoned protein-carb mix for strength and satiety.

Ingredients
1 tbsp rice
1 tbsp moong dal
1 cup water

Instructions
Rinse and pressure cook both with water until soft. Mash or blend into a smooth porridge.




Breakfast Recipe
Suji (Semolina) Porridge

Description
A warm, creamy porridge that’s gentle on the stomach.

Ingredients
1 tbsp semolina (suji)
¾ cup water or milk

Instructions
Dry roast suji until light golden. Add water or milk slowly while stirring to avoid lumps. Simmer until smooth and thick. Cool before serving.
