Technology

Objectives
➧ Learn to talk about technology using the present continuous and simple present tenses.
➧ Expand vocabulary related to common technology terms and devices.


Grammar Focus

Present Continuous Tense for Technology Use

Examples
➧ I am using my phone to send a message.
➧ She is browsing the internet.
➧ They are watching videos online.

This tense helps us describe actions that are currently in progress.


Grammar Focus

Simple Present Tense for Regular Tech Habits

Examples
➧ I check my email every morning.
➧ He uses social media daily.
➧ We often play video games in the evening.

This tense is great for describing how often or when you usually use technology.


Vocabulary

Technology-Related Words

➧ Internet
➧ Wi-Fi
➧ App
➧ Computer
➧ Smartphone
➧ Tablet
➧ Social media
➧ Software
➧ Hardware


Vocabulary

Common Phrases for Discussing Technology

Examples
➧ I’m downloading a new app.
➧ She’s posting a photo on social media.
➧ They’re installing new software on their computer.

These phrases are useful when discussing what people are doing with technology right now.
