4-Week Toddler Dinner Plan

Week 2 | Wholesome, Colorful, and Fuss-Free Dinners

Comforting, colorful, and toddler-approved meals for stress-free evenings.




Dinner Recipe
Pasta with Tomato & Cheese Sauce
A toddler-friendly version of a favorite classic.

Ingredients
¼ cup cooked small pasta
2 tbsp tomato purée (no salt/sugar)
1 tbsp shredded cheese

Instructions
1. Mix pasta with warm tomato sauce and cheese.
2. Stir until melted and serve.




Dinner Recipe
Broccoli & Cheddar Mashed Potatoes
Creamy and mild with hidden veggies.

Ingredients
¼ cup steamed broccoli, finely chopped
½ small potato, peeled and boiled
1 tbsp shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions
1. Mash the potato and stir in cheese and broccoli.
2. Serve warm and soft.




Dinner Recipe
Soft Chicken & Spinach Wrap
Easy-to-hold finger food.

Ingredients
2 tbsp finely shredded cooked chicken
1 tbsp finely chopped spinach
½ small soft tortilla

Instructions
1. Mix chicken and spinach and warm in a pan.
2. Place in tortilla, fold gently, and cut into toddler-sized pieces.




Dinner Recipe
Mashed Chickpeas & Carrot Patties
Protein-packed and finger-friendly.

Ingredients
¼ cup canned chickpeas (no salt), mashed
1 tbsp finely grated carrot
1 tsp oat flour or breadcrumbs

Instructions
1. Mix all and form mini patties.
2. Pan-fry in a little olive oil until golden on both sides.




Dinner Recipe
Mini Meatballs with Couscous
Iron-rich and fluffy.

Ingredients
¼ cup ground beef or turkey
Pinch of garlic powder (optional)
¼ cup cooked couscous

Instructions
1. Roll meat into small balls and bake or pan-fry.
2. Serve with soft, warm couscous.




Dinner Recipe
Mac & Cheese with Hidden Veggies
A toddler classic with a twist.

Ingredients
¼ cup cooked elbow pasta
1 tbsp grated zucchini
1 tbsp shredded cheese
1 tbsp milk

Instructions
1. Sauté zucchini until soft.
2. Stir into warm pasta with cheese and milk until melted and creamy.




Dinner Recipe
Flaky White Fish & Mashed Peas
Light and nutritious.

Ingredients
2 tbsp cooked white fish (cod or tilapia), flaked
¼ cup steamed peas, mashed
1 tsp olive oil

Instructions
1. Mix mashed peas with flaked fish.
2. Add olive oil for softness and serve.
