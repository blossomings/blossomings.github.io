Structure of a Research Paper


Title:
Concise and descriptive.

Abstract:
Summary of the research question, methods, results, and conclusions.

Introduction:
Background, research problem, and study purpose.

Methodology:
Research design, data collection, and analysis methods.

Results:
Presentation of findings with tables and figures.

Discussion:
Interpretation, comparison with existing research, and limitations.

Conclusion:
Summary, implications, and future research.

References:
List of cited sources.


Writing the Introduction

Context:
Background information.

Problem Statement:
Define the research problem.

Objectives:
State research goals.

Methodology

Design:
Research type and approach.

Data Collection:
Methods used.
