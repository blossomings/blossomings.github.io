Daily Routines

Objectives
➧ Use daily routine vocabulary
➧ Talk about your daily life using the present simple
➧ Write and say full sentences like "I wake up at 7:00."


Vocabulary – Daily Actions



Grammar – Present Simple

➧ I wake up at 7:00.
➧ You go to work.
➧ We eat dinner at 6.

Grammar tip
No -s for I · You, We & They

➧ He gets up at 6:30.
➧ She works in an office.
➧ He eats lunch at 12.

Grammar tip
Add -s to the verb for He & She

Tip
➧ watch - watches
➧ go - goes
➧ do - does
