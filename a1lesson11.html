Objectives
• Name common food and drinks
• Talk about meals and your favorite foods
• Use “like/don’t like” to describe food preferences

Meals and Meal Times

Breakfast – in the morning
Lunch – in the afternoon
Dinner – in the evening
Snack – between meals

Example Sentences:
I eat eggs for breakfast.
I drink tea in the morning.
She eats lunch at 1:00.

Like - Don’t Like

I like apples.
I don’t like fish.
He likes chocolate.
She doesn’t like coffee.

Tip Box:
Use like/likes + food
Use don’t/doesn’t + like for negatives
