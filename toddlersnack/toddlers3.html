4-Week Toddler Snack Plan

Week 3 | Natural Treats & Simple Joys

Wholesome snacks designed for toddlers aged 12 months and up.




Snack Recipe
Mini Pancake with Fruit
A handheld treat that feels like breakfast.

Ingredients
1 mini pancake
Small banana slices or berries

Instructions
1. Top pancake with soft fruit.
2. Fold or cut into quarters.




Snack Recipe
Soft-Boiled Egg & Toast Fingers
Easy to dip and eat.

Ingredients
1 soft-boiled egg
1 slice whole grain toast (no crust)

Instructions
1. Cut toast into fingers.
2. Dip in egg or serve side by side.




Snack Recipe
Cottage Cheese & Peaches
Mild and lightly sweet.

Ingredients
2 tbsp cottage cheese
2 tbsp diced soft peach (fresh or canned, no syrup)

Instructions
1. Mix or serve side by side.




Snack Recipe
Mashed Sweet Potato Snack Cup
Warm and comforting.

Ingredients
¼ cup mashed cooked sweet potato
1 tsp butter

Instructions
1. Warm sweet potato with butter.
2. Let cool and serve in a small cup.




Snack Recipe
Tiny Cheese Quesadilla
A mini version of a favorite meal.

Ingredients
½ soft tortilla
2 tbsp shredded mild cheese

Instructions
1. Heat tortilla with cheese until melted.
2. Cut into soft wedges.
